Guidelines:

1. Register your account with a log-in and password in "Sign Up"
TIP: Use a simple username and password that YOU can remember
(there is no automatic password recovery)
2. Log in with your credentials in "Sign In"
3. After a training session,
enter your workouts by selecting either "Shoulders" or "Back" or "Body Weight"
(currently only these section work, but more coming soon!)
4. You can read saved records by pressing "Show All Records"
5. Yet to come: more workout sections (running, beach trainings, etc),
visual dispay of records, and more..


About the site:

This site is coded in Python 3.7 and uses Python library Flask and database PostgreSQL.
Welcome to contribute at https://github.com/adikele/gym-workouts-app.

Site is named after Kelekar family's cat, Luku, who could do 100 pullups in a day.
